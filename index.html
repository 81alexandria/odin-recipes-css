Irish Stew

Nothing is more warming and filling than a delicious bowl of Irish stew, a popular dish from Ireland and loved the world over. It was traditionally made with mutton, but it is now often made with the easier-to-find lamb. Either meat will make a delicious, hearty stew.

Soy Butter Mushroom Pasta

Whip up a delicious pasta dish using ingredients from your storecupboard. Stir your spaghetti and mushrooms into a silky sauce made from soy sauce, butter and garlic.

Chicken Burgers

Turn your kid's favourite meal into a healthier lunchtime treat with these chicken burgers. Wholemeal rolls are filled with grilled chicken, mayonnaise and veggies. Serve with cucumber batons for a kid-approved lunch.
