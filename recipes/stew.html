Irish Stew

Description

Nothing is more warming and filling than a delicious bowl of Irish stew, a popular dish from Ireland and loved the world over. It was traditionally made with mutton, but it is now often made with the easier-to-find lamb. Either meat will make a delicious, hearty stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb chops
  • 3 large onions
  • 1 kg potatoes peeled and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C.
  2. Arrange a layer of chops in a large casserole dish.
  3. Season with cracked pepper.
  4. Cover with a layer of onions and then potatoes.
  5. Repeat until all is used.
  6. Sprinkle the Worcestershire over the top, then pour in enough water to come twothirds up the dish.
  7. Cover and bake for 2 hours.
  8. Remove the lid, and bake for 30 minutes more or until the chops are nice and tender.
